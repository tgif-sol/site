Brainstorming

    1. If you were forced to increase your prices by 10x, what would you have to do to justify it?
    2. If all your customers vanished, and you had to earn your growth and brand from scratch, what would you do?
    3. If you were never allowed to provide tech support, in any form, what would have to change?
    4. What would be the most fun thing to build?
    5. If your biggest competitor copied every single feature, how do you still win?
    6. What if you are forced to ship a full, completed (at least MVP) new feature, in just two weeks, that would delight and surprise some fraction of your customers?
    7. If you only had 2 hours a week to work on this, what would you spend it on?
    8. What if you were forced to charge customers in a completely different manner?
    9. If you were not allowed to have a website, how would you still grow your business?
    10. What if you made your most introverted teammates' dreams come true: No more synchronous meetings, ever again?
    11. If you could never talk to our customers again, how would you figure out what to build?
    12. What if it didn't matter how unprofitable you were?
    13. What if you could change anything, regardless of what anyone thinks or feels?
    14. What externality has the potential to kill the entire company?
    15. What if you could only ship one thing this year?
    16. If you do need to pivot, what is the most likely alternative business model you will be considering?
    17. If you had to go from start to finish in half the time, what would you do? If you could afford to spend double the time on it, what would you do?
